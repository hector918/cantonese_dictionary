Cantonese dictionary with English romanization

This app is inspired by Kimberlina, and built by H.

To honor and remember the cantonese (many from canton/guang dong, toisan, china) ancestors who contributed greatly to the building of the U.S. Transcontinental railroad & developed chinatowns worldwide. Let's pass on cantonese/toisan to the next generation. Our language deserves a place in this world.
